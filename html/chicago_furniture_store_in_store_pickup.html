Chicago Furniture Store In-Store Pickup

If you like shopping online and have a friend with a truck, try out our in-store pickup option! As you browse our extensive collection of handmade furniture on our beautifully designed and organized website, you can rest assured that you’ll be able to pick it up in store no problem.

When you check out, simply select in-store pickup as your delivery option. After you have placed your order, you can pick up from our Lakeview Chicago furniture store at 3036 N. Lincoln. You can come by any time we’re open, which is Tuesday through Sunday 10am to 6pm. However, on Monday we are closed.

There is a free 15-minute loading zone just a few steps south of the store that you can use when you pick up your furniture. We encourage you to bring some blankets to protect your furniture and your car. Your brand nwq furniture pieace will be ready to pick up from our Lakeview furniture store the same or next day from when you order it.

And the best part - we offer free pickup!
